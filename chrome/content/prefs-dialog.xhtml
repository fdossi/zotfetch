⚙ ZotFetch – Preferences

Open Access APIs

Required by Unpaywall, CrossRef, OpenAlex and Europe PMC polite-pool APIs. Your address is only sent as an API identifier — no marketing emails are sent.

Free key at core.ac.uk/services/api — raises rate limit to 10,000 req/day and unlocks download URLs.

Institutional Access

ZotFetch wraps every DOI URL through this address so requests arrive authenticated by your institution’s subscription. Works on-campus, via VPN, or with cookie-authenticated EZproxy sessions. Leave empty if your institution is not supported.

Supported URL formats & examples

EZproxy — most common in universities

  • https://proxy.uni.edu/login?url=
  • https://eresources.uni.edu/login?qurl=
  • https://proxy.uni.edu/login?qurl={url} (ZotFetch replaces {url} automatically)
  • https://proxy.uni.edu (bare base — ZotFetch appends /login?url=)

How to find your proxy URL

  • Search your library website for “off-campus access” or “remote access”
  • The URL usually contains words like proxy, login, or ezproxy
  • Copy the part up to and including ?url= or ?qurl=

The authenticated CAPES gateway URL provided by your institution’s library. Leave empty to hit doi.org directly (works only from a CAPES-connected network or active browser session).

Common CAPES gateway URLs

CAFe / CAPES Periódicos — ask your library which one applies

  • https://capes.ez.no/login?url= — generic CAFe federated login
  • https://www-periodicos-capes-gov-br.ez29.capes.gov.br/index.php?option=com_pemetabusca&mn=not&smn=not&base=find-in-lots-of-places&type=e&Itemid=141&url=
  • Your institution may also provide a personalised EZproxy URL that already routes through CAPES — use Institutional Proxy above in that case
ℹ CAPES only runs in Batch Download (2nd pass / fallback) and Retry Failed — not in Ultra Fast mode.
